Problems

in exisitng system of UOS

In any institute, uniforms represent unity and pride. When it comes to educational institutes, the uniform is a mandatory principle for the students. Many students neglect the policy to wear proper uniforms. It causes an additional responsibility on the staff to keep check and balance of all the students. Even if we hire a coordinator to perform this specific action, this will increase the manpower and expenses for the institute.

Soltuions

By keeping this problem in view and using the power of computer science we intend to propose a system that will help to achieve these objectives:

• Instilling discipline among students.
• Reducing the need for the administrators and faculty to be ‘clothes police’.
• Reducing distractions for students.
• Instilling a sense of community.
• Helping institutions recognize those who do not belong on campus.
